DESCRIPTION OF THE GAME :
  --> In this game you are a Hunter !
  --> Your goal is to shoot as many Ducks as you can.
  --> They will appear randomly on your screen and by just clicking on them, you earn points.
  --> Don't loose too much bullet or you loose ! (MAX 3 bullets)
  --> Try to get the Highest Score :D
  --> Win the round by killing 10 of them.
  --> You can choose between 3 levels ! (vv Look at USEFUL INFORMATION vv)

    !!! SHOOT THEM ALL !!!

USEFUL INFORMATIONS :
  --> Use "./my_hunter" or "./my_hunter 1" to launch the game in level 1.
  --> Usa "./my_hunter 2" to launch the game in level 2;
  --> Usa "./my_hunter 3" to launch the game in level 3;
  --> Use "./my_hunter -h" for help.
  --> Press ESCAPE key to leave the game at any moment.
